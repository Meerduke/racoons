The mammal with the adorable face

The racoon, sometimes called the common racoon or Trash Panda to distinguish it from other species, is a medium-sized mammal native to North America. It is the largest of the procyonid family, having a body length of 40 to 70 cm, and a body weight of 5 to 26 kg.
